VentureMind is the agent swarm powering **Nomad Flow** — a global SaaS platform for founders and entrepreneurs who need AI-guided product development, international business formation, lifestyle infrastructure, and a vetted human network.

The company operates as a **Swarm of Swarms**: 10 autonomous domain swarms coordinated by a central orchestrator, each with a Domain Lead agent and specialist sub-agents. All actions are governed by SafetyNet protocols with human-in-the-loop checkpoints for high-stakes decisions.

**Governance Model**: All swarms report to the **Central Swarm Lead**. High-risk actions (>$10,000, legal filings, key rotations) require explicit Houdinnie authorisation via the Green Button protocol. An independent **Watchdog Agent** monitors all activity and enforces escalation triggers.

**Budget Model**: Each swarm has a monthly compute budget enforced by Paperclip. SafetyNet operations are budget-exempt.
