The Deep Discovery Ingestion project is the first interaction a founder has with VentureMind. It runs the 7-stage adaptive intake form driven by the Strategy Agent, produces a verified FounderProfile, and triggers the 4 parallel verification loops.

**Stages:**

1. Identity & Jurisdiction
2. Business Model
3. Capital Profile
4. Legal Goals
5. Mobility Intent
6. Tech Readiness
7. Risk Tolerance

**Exit criteria:** FounderProfile with all 7 stages complete, confidence scores assigned, KYC tier determined, SafetyNet disclaimer signed.
