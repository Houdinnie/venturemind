The Green Button Execution project transforms the FounderBlueprint from research into real-world action. Every execution manifest is cryptographically signed, passes through SafetyNet triggers, and requires explicit Houdinnie authorisation for CRIMSON and RED risk actions.

**Manifest types:**
- EXEC-ENTITY-FORMATION: Legal entity creation (CRIMSON)
- EXEC-NEOBANK-FIAT: Bank account setup (RED)
- EXEC-SOVEREIGN-ACCOUNT: Multi-sig crypto wallet (CRIMSON)
- EXEC-TAX-STRUCTURE: Tax optimisation implementation (RED)
- EXEC-CAPITAL-RAISE: Fundraise execution (CRIMSON)
