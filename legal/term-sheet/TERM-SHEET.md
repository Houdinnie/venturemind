## 3. ANTI-DILUTION

### 3.1 Broad-Based Weighted Average
In the event of a down round (i.e., a subsequent equity financing at a price per share lower than the price paid by the Investor), the conversion price of the Preferred Shares shall be adjusted using the broad-based weighted average formula:

```
Adjusted Price = (Old Price × (Old Shares + Safe Amount / Old Price)) / (Old Shares + New Shares)
```

### 3.2 Full Ratchet Exception
[ ] Full ratchet applies to Series ___ in the event of a down round.
[ ] No full ratchet — weighted average only.

### 4.1 Preference Amount

1. The Investment Amount multiplied by [__] ("Liquidation Multiple"); or

(the "Preference Amount").

### 4.2 Definition of Liquidation Event
A "Liquidation Event" means:
1. A merger, acquisition, or consolidation of the Company with or into another entity;
2. A sale, transfer, or other disposition of all or substantially all of the Company's assets;
3. A sale of a majority of the Company's outstanding voting securities;
4. A dissolution or winding up of the Company;
5. A deemed liquidation event as defined in the Shareholders' Agreement.
