## RECITALS

**WHEREAS**, the Assignor is or may be involved in the development, creation, or conception of intellectual property in connection with the Company or the VentureMind/Nomad Flow platform;

## SECTION 1 — ASSIGNMENT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 1.1 Full Assignment
The Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns to the Company, its successors, licensees, and assigns, throughout the world, for the full term of copyright and any extensions or renewals thereof:

1. All intellectual property developed, created, conceived, or reduced to practice by the Assignor, whether alone or jointly with others, in connection with the Company or the Platform during the period of the Assignor's engagement or involvement;
2. All rights, title, and interests in and to all such intellectual property, including all underlying rights;
3. All causes of action for past, present, and future infringement, misappropriation, or violation of such intellectual property;
4. All registrations, applications, and renewals relating to such intellectual property.

### 1.2 Scope of Assigned IP
This assignment covers, without limitation:
- Source code, software, APIs, databases, and documentation;
- Designs, user interfaces, graphics, and branding;
- Business methods, processes, workflows, and operational know-how;
- Training materials, templates, and derivative works;
- Any improvements, modifications, or enhancements to any pre-existing IP of the Assignor;
- All drafts, notes, prototypes, and intermediate materials.

### 1.3 No Reservation
The Assignor expressly reserves no rights whatsoever in any of the assigned intellectual property. All intellectual property assigned hereunder shall be and remain the exclusive property of the Company.

### 1.4 Moral Rights
To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, the Assignor irrevocably waives any and all moral rights (including rights of attribution, integrity, and the right to object to derogatory treatment) in and to all assigned intellectual property. Where such waiver is not permitted by applicable law, the Assignor grants the Company an exclusive, perpetual, irrevocable licence to exercise all moral rights in all relevant jurisdictions.

## SECTION 2 — PRE-EXISTING INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 2.1 Schedule of Pre-Existing IP
The Assignor acknowledges that certain intellectual property may have been developed or acquired prior to the Effective Date ("Pre-Existing IP"). A complete schedule of any Pre-Existing IP is attached as **Schedule A**. The Assignor represents and warrants that all Pre-Existing IP is:
1. Wholly owned by the Assignor and free of encumbrances;
2. Not subject to any licence, claim, or restriction that would prevent its use by the Company;
3. Clearly identified and described in Schedule A.

### 2.2 Grant of Licence for Pre-Existing IP
To the extent that any Pre-Existing IP is incorporated into or used in connection with the Platform, the Assignor hereby grants to the Company a perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free, worldwide, exclusive licence to use, reproduce, modify, and distribute such Pre-Existing IP for the purpose of operating the Platform.

### 2.3 No Challenge
The Assignor shall not challenge the validity, ownership, or enforceability of any intellectual property assigned or licensed under this Agreement.

---

## SECTION 3 — FURTHER ASSURANCES

### 3.1 Documentation
The Assignor shall, at the Company's expense, execute all documents and take all actions reasonably requested by the Company to perfect, register, record, or enforce the assignments and licences granted under this Agreement.

### 3.2 Timing
The Assignor shall respond to any request by the Company for execution of additional documents within fourteen (14) days of receiving such request.

### 3.3 Power of Attorney
The Assignor hereby grants the Company a limited power of attorney, coupled with an interest, to execute any document or take any action on behalf of the Assignor that the Assignor fails to take within the timeframe specified in Section 3.2, for the purpose of perfecting the assignments under this Agreement.

---

## SECTION 4 — INDEMNIFICATION

### 4.1 Indemnification by Assignor
The Assignor shall indemnify and hold harmless the Company from any third-party claim arising from:
1. Any Pre-Existing IP that is alleged to infringe, misappropriate, or violate any third party's intellectual property rights;
2. Any breach of the Assignor's representations and warranties under this Agreement.

### 4.2 Indemnification Procedure
The Company shall notify the Assignor of any claim covered by Section 4.1 within thirty (30) days of becoming aware of it. The Assignor shall have the right to control the defence of any such claim, provided that the Company shall have the right to participate in such defence at its own expense.

---

## SECTION 5 — REMEDIES

The Assignor acknowledges that breach of this Agreement would cause the Company irreparable harm not compensable by monetary damages alone. The Company shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief and specific performance in addition to all other available remedies.
