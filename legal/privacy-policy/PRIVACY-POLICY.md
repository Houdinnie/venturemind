## 6. DATA SECURITY

### 6.1 Technical and Organisational Measures
We implement the following security measures:
1. AES-256 encryption at rest; TLS 1.3 encryption in transit;
2. Zero-knowledge architecture where technically feasible;
3. Role-based access controls and least-privilege principles;
4. Multi-factor authentication for all internal systems;
5. Regular penetration testing and vulnerability assessments;
6. Annual ISO 27001 / SOC 2 Type II audits;
7. TEE (Trusted Execution Environment) for the most sensitive operations;
8. Immutable audit logs for all data access and modifications.

### 6.2 Incident Response
In the event of a data breach, we shall:
1. Notify the relevant supervisory authority within seventy-two (72) hours of becoming aware;
2. Notify affected individuals without undue delay if the breach is likely to result in a high risk to their rights and freedoms;
3. Document all facts of the breach, its effects, and remedial actions taken.

### 6.3 Your Responsibilities
You are responsible for maintaining the security of your own account credentials and for promptly notifying us of any suspected breach.
