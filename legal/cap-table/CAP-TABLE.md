## SECTION 7 — TRANSFER RESTRICTIONS

### 7.1 Lock-Up
No Shareholder may transfer, assign, or otherwise dispose of any shares without the prior written consent of the Board. Any purported transfer in violation of this provision shall be void.

### 7.2 Right of First Refusal (ROFR)
Prior to any proposed transfer, the transferring Shareholder must offer their shares to the other Shareholders pro-rata at the same price and on the same terms as the proposed transfer. The existing Shareholders have thirty (30) days to exercise the ROFR.

### 7.3 Tag-Along Rights
If a Shareholder transfers more than [__]% of their shares to a third party, the other Shareholders have the right to participate in the transfer and sell a pro-rata portion of their shares at the same price and on the same terms.

### 7.4 Drag-Along Rights
If holders of at least [__]% of all shares approve a trade sale or merger, all remaining Shareholders shall be required to sell their shares at the same price and on the same terms.
