## 1. PURPOSE

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means all non-public information, in any form, disclosed by the Disclosing Party to the Receiving Party, whether before or after the Effective Date, including but not limited to:

- Trade secrets, source code, algorithms, technical architecture, and product roadmaps;
- Business plans, financial data, pricing models, and customer lists;
- The existence and terms of this Agreement and any potential transaction;
- Any information marked "Confidential" or "Proprietary."

**Confidential Information does not include information that:**
1. Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party;
2. Was lawfully in the Receiving Party's possession before disclosure;
3. Is independently developed without reference to Confidential Information;
4. Is received from a third party without breach of confidentiality obligations.

---

## 3. OBLIGATIONS OF THE RECEIVING PARTY

The Receiving Party agrees to:

1. Maintain all Confidential Information in strict confidence;
2. Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose described in Section 1;
3. Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent;
4. Protect Confidential Information using at least the same degree of care as for its own most sensitive information (and no less than reasonable care);
5. Limit disclosure to employees and advisors who need to know and are bound by equivalent obligations;
6. Return or destroy all Confidential Information upon written request or upon termination of discussions;
7. Promptly notify the Disclosing Party of any suspected breach.

---

## 4. PERMITTED DISCLOSURES

The Receiving Party may disclose Confidential Information to the extent required by:

1. Applicable law, regulation, or court order, provided that the Receiving Party:
   a. Gives the Disclosing Party prior written notice (where permitted by law);
   b. Cooperates with the Disclosing Party's efforts to seek confidential treatment;
   c. Discloses only the minimum required amount;
2. The Compliance Auditor Agent of the VentureMind Platform, subject to execution of appropriate data protection agreements.

## 5. OWNERSHIP OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

All Confidential Information remains the sole property of the Disclosing Party. No licence, ownership interest, or other rights are granted by this Agreement.

## 6. TERM

This Agreement shall remain in effect for three (3) years from the Effective Date. Confidentiality obligations shall survive for an indefinite period with respect to trade secrets and for five (5) years from disclosure with respect to all other Confidential Information.

---

## 7. REMEDIES

The Receiving Party acknowledges that breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages may be inadequate. The Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief and specific performance in addition to any other remedies available at law.
