## RECITALS

---

## SECTION 1 — GRANT OF OPTIONS

### 1.1 Grant
Subject to the terms of this Agreement, the Company hereby grants to the Option Holder an option (the "Option") to subscribe for _______________ ordinary shares in the Company (the "Option Shares") at an exercise price of $__________ per share (the "Exercise Price").

### 1.2 Grant Date
The Option is granted as of _______________ (the "Grant Date").

### 1.3 Nature of Option
The Option is a right to subscribe for shares in the Company and does not entitle the Option Holder to any shareholder rights (including voting rights or dividend rights) until and unless the Option is exercised and the Option Shares are issued.

---

## SECTION 2 — VESTING SCHEDULE

### 2.1 Standard Vesting
The Option shall vest in accordance with the following schedule:

| Period | Cumulative Vested % |
|--------|---------------------|
| Prior to first anniversary of Grant Date | 0% |
| First anniversary of Grant Date (Cliff) | 25% |
| Months 13–24 (monthly) | 6.25% per month |
| Month 24 (fully vested) | 100% |

### 2.2 Continuous Service Required
Vesting requires the Option Holder to remain in continuous service to the Company throughout the vesting period. Any break in service exceeding thirty (30) consecutive days shall toll the vesting clock for the period of the break, unless the break is statutorily mandated (e.g., maternity leave, disability leave).

### 2.3 Acceleration on Change of Control
Upon a Change of Control (as defined in the Founder Agreement), all unvested options shall vest immediately and become exercisable.

---

## SECTION 3 — EXERCISE OF OPTIONS

### 3.1 Exercise Window
The Option Holder may exercise the Option at any time after vesting and before the Expiration Date (as defined in Section 4).

### 3.2 Exercise Notice
To exercise the Option, the Option Holder must deliver a written notice of exercise (the "Exercise Notice") to the Company Secretary, specifying:
1. The number of Option Shares to be subscribed;
2. Payment of the aggregate Exercise Price in full;
3. Execution of any documents required by the Company in connection with the exercise.

### 3.3 Payment Methods
The aggregate Exercise Price must be paid in full by:
- Wire transfer to the Company's designated bank account;
- [Alternative methods if applicable: _______________]

Cash-only payment. Barter, credit, or off-setting of claims against the Company is not permitted as a method of payment.

### 3.4 Issuance of Shares
Within thirty (30) days of receipt of a valid Exercise Notice and payment, the Company shall issue the Option Shares to the Option Holder and update the Share Register accordingly.

---

## SECTION 4 — EXPIRATION

### 4.1 Expiration Date
The Option shall expire and lapse automatically on the earliest of:
1. The tenth (10th) anniversary of the Grant Date;
2. Ninety (90) days after the Option Holder's termination of service for any reason other than death or disability;
3. Twelve (12) months after the Option Holder's death or disability, if the Option Holder (or their estate) does not exercise the Option during such period.

### 4.2 No Extension
The Expiration Date shall not be extended under any circumstances except by written agreement signed by both Parties and approved by the Company's Board.

---

## SECTION 5 — LAPSE OF OPTIONS

### 5.1 Automatic Lapse
The Option (whether vested or unvested) shall lapse automatically, with no consideration payable, if:
1. The Option Holder commits a material breach of their employment or service agreement with the Company;
2. The Option Holder is dismissed for cause;
3. The Option Holder materially breaches any confidentiality, IP assignment, non-compete, or non-solicitation obligation;
4. The Option Holder engages in conduct that causes material harm to the Company's reputation or business;
5. The Option Holder fails to exercise the Option before the Expiration Date.

### 5.2 No Compensation
Lapse of the Option under Section 5.1 shall not entitle the Option Holder to any compensation, payment, or consideration from the Company.

---

## SECTION 6 — LEAVING THE COMPANY

### 6.1 Resignation Without Good Reason
If the Option Holder resigns without Good Reason (as defined in the Founder Agreement):
- Vested options must be exercised within sixty (60) days of the last day of service;
- All unvested options lapse immediately on the last day of service.

### 6.2 Termination Without Cause
If the Option Holder's service is terminated Without Cause:
- All unvested options continue to vest for a period of [__] months following termination (subject to the option's original Expiration Date);
- Vested options may be exercised at any time before the original Expiration Date.

### 6.3 Termination With Cause
If the Option Holder's service is terminated With Cause:
- All unvested options lapse immediately;
- Vested options must be exercised within thirty (30) days of termination.

### 6.4 Death or Disability
If the Option Holder dies or becomes disabled:
- All unvested options vest immediately;
- The Option (or its estate equivalent) may be exercised within twelve (12) months of death or disability.

---

## SECTION 7 — TAXATION

### 7.1 Responsibility
The Option Holder is solely responsible for all taxes, national insurance contributions, and similar charges arising from the grant, vesting, and exercise of the Option (the "Tax Liabilities").

### 7.2 Withholding
The Company is authorised to withhold from any amounts payable to the Option Holder (including from the exercise proceeds) any taxes required to be withheld by applicable law. If insufficient cash is available, the Company may require the Option Holder to pay the withheld amount directly.

### 7.3 No Tax Advice
The Company does not provide tax advice. The Option Holder should obtain independent tax advice before exercising any options.

---

## SECTION 8 — TRANSFERABILITY

### 8.1 Prohibition
The Option is personal to the Option Holder and may not be transferred, assigned, mortgaged, pledged, encumbered, or otherwise disposed of without the prior written consent of the Company. Any purported transfer in violation of this provision shall be void.

### 8.2 Exception — Death
Upon the Option Holder's death, the Option may be transferred to the Option Holder's estate, subject to the terms of this Agreement and applicable law.

---

## SECTION 9 — COMPANY REPRESENTATIONS

The Company represents and warrants that:
1. It has the authority to grant the Option and issue the Option Shares;
2. The Option Shares, when issued, will be fully paid, non-assessable, and free of encumbrances (except as arising by operation of law);
3. The issuance of the Option Shares has been duly authorised by all necessary corporate action;
4. The Company's share capital is as set out in the Cap Table, and there are no outstanding rights, options, or commitments to issue shares other than those disclosed herein.
